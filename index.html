Frequently Asked Questions

How to split a PDF file online?

To split a PDF file online with PDFSplit, simply upload your PDF file, choose your preferred split mode (split by page count, split by page ranges, or extract specific pages), and click the "Split PDF" button. Your PDF will be split in seconds.

Is PDFSplit free to use?

Yes, PDFSplit is completely free to use. You can split as many PDF files as you need without any limitations.

Is my PDF file secure when using PDFSplit?

Absolutely! All PDF processing is done locally in your browser, which means your files never leave your computer. PDFSplit does not upload your files to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What is the maximum file size supported?

PDFSplit supports PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, we recommend compressing your PDF first or splitting it into smaller parts.

Can I split password-protected PDF files?

No, currently PDFSplit does not support splitting password-protected or encrypted PDF files. You will need to decrypt the PDF file first before using our tool.

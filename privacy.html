Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2023

PDFSplit takes your privacy and data security very seriously. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain how we handle your data when using our online PDF splitting tool and what measures we take to protect your privacy.

Our Privacy Principles

At PDFSplit, we follow these privacy principles:

  • Local Processing: All PDF file processing is done locally in your browser and is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Zero Storage: We do not store any of your uploaded PDF files or processing results.
  • Data Security: We implement strict security measures to protect your privacy and data security.
  • Transparency: We clearly explain how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

PDFSplit does not collect or store any content from your uploaded PDF files. We may collect the following types of non-personal information:

  • Usage Statistics: We may collect information about how you use our tool, such as number of visits, features used, etc., but this information is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Browser Information: We may collect technical information such as your browser type and operating system to optimize our service.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use, such as device type and screen resolution.

This information is used solely to improve our service and is not linked to your personal identity.

How We Use Collected Information

The non-personal information we collect is used only for the following purposes:

  • Improving the performance and functionality of our PDF splitting tool
  • Optimizing user experience
  • Analyzing tool usage patterns
  • Resolving technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or transfer your information to any third parties.

Local Processing Technical Explanation

PDFSplit uses advanced browser technology to ensure all PDF processing is done locally on your computer. This means:

  • Your PDF files never leave your computer
  • File contents are not uploaded to any servers
  • Processing results are generated directly in your browser
  • You can use our tool without an internet connection (some features may be limited)

This processing method ensures the security and privacy of your file contents while also improving processing speed.

Cookie Policy

PDFSplit may use cookies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that remember your preferences and settings.

The cookies we use are primarily for:

  • Remembering your language preferences
  • Remembering your recently used split settings
  • Analyzing website traffic and usage patterns

Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of our tool.

Third-Party Services

PDFSplit may use some third-party services to help us provide and improve our service. These services may collect information about your use of our tool.

The main third-party services we use include:

  • Google Analytics: For analyzing website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Google Tag Manager: For managing website tags.
  • CDN Services: For providing faster resource loading speeds.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend that you review these policies to understand how they handle your information.

Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your privacy and data security:

  • All data processing is done locally in your browser
  • HTTPS protocol is used to ensure secure data transmission
  • Regular updates and maintenance of our systems
  • Compliance with industry best practices and standards

Children's Privacy

PDFSplit is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will post the updated Privacy Policy on our website and update the "Last Updated" date. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically to understand how we are protecting your privacy.
